Digital Art Freelancing Career Consultation
My career consultation program provides customized solutions and actionable advice for your freelance career: portfolio-building, client-acquisition, project management, rate negotiations, payment hiccups, entering multiple industries, dealing with unsupportive family members, and more.

This won’t be your usual motivational fluff nor would this be about leaving you inspired for a week. This is about giving you useful, applicable tactics to help boost your career. I’ll be sharing my in-depth experience managing more than 120 freelance creatives through countless projects, milestones, and hurdles in my past 15 years in the industry.

My Mission
I am here to help create better professionals to represent our industry. With better representation, we can push for healthier collaborations between creatives and non-creatives and redefine the role artists play in them. With enough positive momentum, we can turn Philippines into a powerhouse country for outsourcing art projects to and turn digital art freelancing into a legitimate option for any aspiring creative professional to take.

Consultant Background
Emmanuel "E.M." Javier is CEO and Co-founder of Gunship Revolution, a digital art consultancy company. With fifteen years' experience in the entertainment arts industry with over seven helming an international company with four branches around the world, E.M. Javier has worked with over 120 artists on more than 600 projects for more than 160 clients from more than 20 different countries, and on more than 100 properties. Notable clients include Disney, Marvel, Hasbro, Lucasfilm, Capcom, Bandai Namco, Square Enix, DC Entertainment, SNK, Wizards of the Coast, and Mattel. Notable projects include working on properties like Transformers, Star Wars, Conan, Street Fighter, Mega Man, G.I. Joe, Elder Scrolls, and Mortal Kombat. As of this writing, the company has provided 8-digits worth of creative project opportunities to Filipino freelancers around the world. Gunship Revolution has also contributed art to several successful crowdfunding campaigns with over $USD15m funds raised.

What will reserving a Consultation Slot get me?

You will get a detailed customized solution set to either launch your career, solve your current hurdles, or upgrade your working conditions. You will receive a detailed outline of all of the topics discussed during the session for reference and to review.
Process
After purchasing and reserving a slot, you will receive a list of options for time, date, and venue.
To prepare for the 1-on-1 consultation, I suggest preparing the following:
1. Your personal and updated list of services, projects, and clients.
2. Your current and target rates for each service.
3. Your target clients (Square Enix, Capcom, Marvel etc.) and properties (Star Wars, Transformers, Final Fantasy etc.).
4. Your current portfolio.
5. A copy of your introduction letter to clients.
6. A list of problems you consistently encounter during projects.
7. A list of objectives you want to achieve in the next month, year, and half decade.

Please make sure to arrive on time and that you’re free from distractions. I suggest keeping your phone on silent.
Frequently Asked Questions
WILL THE CONSULTATION SESSION GUARANTEE THAT I'LL LAND MORE FREELANCE GIGS?
Unfortunately, there are never realistic guarantees in any business. There are far too many factors outside anyone's control for me to confidently say that my method has a 100% success rate. Of course, factors like the quality of your work, your production speed, your current lifestyle, and more play a big role in determining your career’s trajectory. What I can guarantee is advice drawn from years of consistently helping build successful careers customized to fit your current skill level and expectations. At the end of the day, it’ll be up to you what you do with it.

WILL THE CONSULTATION SESSION BE IN ENGLISH?
We can discuss in English and Filipino. More options coming soon.

WHO CAN BENEFIT FROM THIS SESSION?
I welcome any type of creative who is interested in diving into our line of work. I’m also open to meeting with students who want to get an early start in preparing for freelancing life or entering any of the entertainment industries we’re currently work in.

CAN I DO MULTIPLE SESSIONS TO ENSURE MY PROGRESS?
Yes, I can do multiple sessions with an individual freelancer. There is good advice to be had at every stage of the process. As long as my advice is needed, I’ll be open for it. I’m aware that all freelancers are built differently and that some might require just one session to experience that eureka moment and some might need multiple to keep the momentum going. I have experience working with both types.

WHAT IF MY SESSION GOES BEYOND MY ALLOTTED SLOT?
If there’s another session scheduled for the day, you’ll have to reserve another slot. If there isn’t, we can work with an extension.
