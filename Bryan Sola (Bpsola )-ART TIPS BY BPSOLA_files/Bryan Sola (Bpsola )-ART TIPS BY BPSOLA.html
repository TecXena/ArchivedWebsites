Hello guys! This is my painting process for my artworks. This file was originally created for my apprentice a few years back, and I realized why not share it to the public as well. Hopefully this helps you in having a well-structured pipeline. I have added the term "Staging" below the Layout/Composition as per Mr. Armand Serrano"s keyword during the Icon manila 2019, just to avoid confusions because technically all of these stages are compositions in their own nature, so credits to him for that one. Just let me know if the subdetails are too small to read, I"ll make a way to post here a high res version for your references. Hope this helps you and enjoy painting awesome stuff!

-Bryan S.
